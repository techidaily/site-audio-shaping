## PinnacleTrack Audio Editor: Comprehensive MP3 Tagging Capabilities for Windows & Mac

ID3 is a metadata container widely used in conjunction with MP3 audio files. This container file is used to store information like artist, track number, album, etc. ID3 tags have two unrelated versions, ID3v1 and ID3v2\. ID3 tags are used for quick identification of the songs, without putting much effort or without even playing the song.

The tags consisting of the information helps in finding the song. If any song does not have ID3 tags, you can add metadata yourself for your ease. For this purpose, MP3 tag editors are available that can be used to add and edit the information. The following article will talk about music tag editors for both Windows and Mac users.
